Memories

The Beginning

The journey begins over three and a half decades ago when a man with a vision began his entrepreneurial journey by establishing a small tea-coffee stall. Over time, the stall witnessed numerous customers as its popularity grew. As years went by, the owner decided to add Samosa to the menu as an add-on for customers to enjoy with their tea or coffee. What started as a complimentary snack eventually led to the rapid success of the stall. And it gained its reputation among the locals as the beloved "Samosa Pasal".

The Journey

As time progressed, Samosa Pasal became the go-to place for snack lovers. Positive feedback and regular visits from customers ultimately resulted in the establishment to be informally known as Tip Top Samosa.

The Growth

At present, we have expanded our offerings from traditional tea/coffee and samosas to a spectacular selection of sweets and snacks. We aim to serve and continue to evolve by introducing additional enhancements to our menu in the future.
