About Us

This is about 35 years back a man started his journey with a tea-coffee stall which became the talk of the town. After a few years, he added something along with tea-coffee, so he started with 10 pcs of samosa, which became the renowned samosa pasal. People started liking the taste and kept coming back to Tip Top for the samosa, which later became Tip Top samosa. Over time, we expanded from tea-coffee-samosa to a wide range of sweets and snacks, along with catering services and much more to come.
