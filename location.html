Sukrapath, New Road

Tip Top Sweets shop in Sukrapath, New Road, Kathmandu. It is a small alleyway off Sukra Path, opposite Bishal Bazar. The shop is not easy to find, but it is worth the effort. They are famous for their samosas and dhokla.
